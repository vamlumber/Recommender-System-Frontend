Goodread Recommender System

Books are still the oldest and best source of knowledge and information. In digital age , all books are available on the internet. The problem is to find the needed book and the books which are similar. Looking at the way netflix and many e-commerce websites do such a fabulous work making user personalized recommender. Over the years avid readers want more insight ways to explore new books.


FEATURES

1. Search:A user will have the ability to search books available based on every attribute ,right till publisher house.
2. Classifer : Will infer the genre the text belongs, for the user who are interested in text and semantics analysis.
3. Recommender :When a used selects any book, we will provide him with recommendation of the similar books.

Dataset :Goodreads dataset
